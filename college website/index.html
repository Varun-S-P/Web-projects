The ISE(Information Science and Engineering) in IT is basically about storing, processing, securing, and managing information. This degree is primarily focused on subjects such as software, databases, and networking.

EEE

The EEE(Electrical and Electronics Engineering) is a broad field that combines electrical and electronics engineering principles. It focuses on the design, development, and application of electrical systems and technologies.

ECE

The ECE(Electronics and Communication Engineering) is a field focused on the design, development, and application of electronic devices and systems for communication purposes. It encompasses a wide range of areas, including circuits, digital and analog communication, signal processing, and embedded systems.

CE

The CE(Civil Engineering) is a professional engineering discipline that deals with the design, construction, and maintenance of the physical and naturally built environment, including public works such as roads, bridges, canals, dams, airports, sewage systems, pipelines, structural components of buildings, and railways.
